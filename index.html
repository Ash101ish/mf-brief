Regulatory Backdrop

The big regulatory shift this season is now in force. SEBI's revised Mutual Fund Regulations 2026 took effect on April 1, and the consolidated 748-page Master Circular issued on March 20 is the single reference document everyone in the industry is now working from.

MFD tagline compliance is live as of May 1. All distributor communication must now carry the AMFI Registered Mutual Fund Distributor (ARN) tagline. Non-compliance attracts regulatory action. No direct impact on your funds, but expect channels you read - including AMC newsletters and broker emails - to look slightly different from this week onward.

Digital platform framework now formalised. Two categories of direct-plan platforms (AMFI-registered and broker-registered) are now formally regulated, with neither permitted to offer regular-plan services. Improves the integrity of the direct-plan channel you actually use.

50% portfolio overlap cap and lower TER slabs from the new regulations continue to filter through. Watch the next round of scheme factsheets for any forced rebalancing notices.
